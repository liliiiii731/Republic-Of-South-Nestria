Entry Regulations

Entry into the country is allowed for citizens of the United States who are exempt from passport requirements, but the following documents are mandatory: a driver's license and a document proving residence.

Persons who are not citizens of the United States must have a passport and also a visa for the United States and a visa for the Republic of South-Nestria. The entry visa for the Republic of South-Nestria is valid for 30 days and costs $25.

The following items are prohibited from being imported into the country, and violating this regulation can have serious consequences:

  • Firearms
  • Ammunition
  • Narcotics
  • Dangerous or endangered animals
  • Pornographic newspapers, magazines, or CDs
  • Currency of other nations, with the exception of the US dollar

Violating the entry regulations or smuggling prohibited items can result in immediate arrest and a prison sentence of up to 5 years.
