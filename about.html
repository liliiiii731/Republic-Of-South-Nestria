About us

The Republic of South-Nestria is a small country located in Eastern Europe. It is a parliamentary republic with a president as its head of state. The country has a population of around 500,000 people, and its capital city is Tiraspol.

The country has a rich history, with influences from various cultures throughout the centuries. Today, it is a vibrant and diverse country, with a strong economy and a thriving tourism industry.
